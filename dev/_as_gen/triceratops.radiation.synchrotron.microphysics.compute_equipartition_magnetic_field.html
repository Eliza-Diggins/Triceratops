triceratops.radiation.synchrotron.microphysics.compute_equipartition_magnetic_field#

triceratops.radiation.synchrotron.microphysics.compute_equipartition_magnetic_field(u_therm: float | ndarray | Quantity, epsilon_B: _ArrayLike) → Quantity[source]#

Compute the magnetic field strength from the thermal energy density and epsilon_B.

If \(\varepsilon_B\) is the fraction of the thermal energy density allocated to magnetic fields, then the magnetic field strength can be computed as

\[B = \sqrt{8 \pi \varepsilon_B u_{\rm int}}.\]
Parameters:

  • u_therm (float, array-like, or astropy.units.Quantity) – Thermal energy density. Default units are erg cm^{-3}, but an astropy.units.Quantity may be provided to use general units.

  • epsilon_B (float or array-like) – Fraction of post-shock energy in magnetic fields. Default is 0.1.

Returns:

Magnetic field strength in Gauss.

Return type:

astropy.units.Quantity
